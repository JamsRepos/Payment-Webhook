Our Payment Plans

If you are wanting to upgrade your Karna.ge plan, you have come to the right place.
We currently support both PayPal & Stripe. You can do this via both a subscription or a one-time payment.
To upgrade your plan, please select the plan you wish to upgrade to and click the button below.

 If you do not use your username in case-sensitive format, your payment will be processed but your perks will not be delivered.

Monthly Subscription

If you would like to pay by a 'set and forget' mentality then this is the package type for you.

Simply set up your subscription and as long as you have money in your account, you will have access to your perks.

One Time Payment

If you would like to be able to pay on a one-time basis, then this is the package type for you.

Simply pay once and access your perks then once the time expires, simply purchase more time.
